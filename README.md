# Privacy Policy for CakeBell

**Last updated: January 20, 2025**

## Overview

CakeBell ("the App") is a birthday reminder application that helps you remember and celebrate your contacts' birthdays. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection and Storage

### What Data We Access
- **Contact Information**: The App reads contact names, birthdays, phone numbers, and email addresses from your device's address book
- **Contact Photos**: Profile pictures from your contacts (if available)
- **User Preferences**: Your settings including reminder times, intervals, and relationship categorizations

### How We Store Data
- **All data is stored locally** on your device using AsyncStorage
- **No cloud storage**: We do not upload or sync your data to any external servers
- **No third-party access**: Your contact information never leaves your device

## Data Usage

We use the collected data solely to:
- Display upcoming birthdays
- Send local notifications for birthday reminders
- Generate personalized birthday messages
- Organize contacts by relationship type

## Data Sharing

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.**

The App only interacts with messaging applications (SMS, WhatsApp, Threema, Telegram) when you explicitly choose to send a birthday message. These interactions are handled directly by the respective apps on your device.

## Permissions

The App requires the following permissions:
- **Contacts**: To read birthday information from your address book
- **Notifications**: To send birthday reminders
- **SMS** (optional): To send text messages through your device's messaging app

## Data Retention

- Contact data is refreshed each time you open the App
- Sent message records are automatically deleted after 24 hours
- User settings remain until you delete the App

## Data Security

All data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's security measures (passcode, biometric authentication, etc.).

## Your Rights

You can:
- Revoke permissions at any time through your device settings
- Delete all App data by uninstalling the application
- Modify relationship types and messaging preferences within the App

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App is designed for general audiences.
